Flagship Build · UAV Hardware

Tailsitter VTOL — Border Surveillance UAV

A 1.36m wingspan tailsitter VTOL UAV engineered for seamless transition between hover and forward flight. Designed for high-endurance border surveillance missions requiring persistent ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance) capability over extended ranges.

The aircraft achieves stable hover using differential thrust, then pitches into fixed-wing forward flight for energy-efficient cruise — eliminating the need for separate lift rotors. Key engineering challenges included transition-phase stability control, composite structural weight optimisation, and Pixhawk-based autopilot integration. Leading the structures team at IISc for physical build and composite layup.
