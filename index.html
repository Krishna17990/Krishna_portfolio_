C.H.I.M.E.R.A – UAV Transformer

designing and rapid prototyping of the CHIMERA transformer UAV, I engineered the critical electro-mechanical integration that drives its morphological capabilities. I developed a high-efficiency power transmission system coupled with a Teensy microcontroller architecture , enabling precise, latency-free communication between the flight computer and heavy-duty actuators. This work demanded a synthesis of structural analysis and embedded logic, where I moved from CAD iterations to physical validation, ensuring the airframe could withstand the dynamic loads of mid-flight transformation while maintaining aerodynamic stability.

  • Conceptual aerodynamic design
  • Stability and transition considerations
  • Research-oriented UAV architecture
